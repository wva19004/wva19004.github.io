Why Food Tech Matters in Malaysia
As Malaysia continues to grow, ensuring sustainable food security becomes increasingly critical. Food technology plays a vital role in developing innovative preservation methods, improving nutritional value, and reducing food waste. By combining traditional Malaysian food knowledge with modern scientific approaches, we can create solutions that not only feed our nation but also preserve our rich culinary heritage for future generations.
